ABOUT
DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

The Department of Mathematics at IIT Bhilai was established in 2016, with a focus on fostering excellence in teaching and high-quality research. Mathematics, as the cornerstone of scientific inquiry, plays a pivotal role in addressing global challenges and unifying various branches of science and technology.
Since its inception, the department has prioritized the production of engineers equipped with modern mathematical tools and a profound understanding of real-world and engineering problems. Emphasizing high-quality research, the department provides a nurturing environment conducive to scholarly inquiry. Esteemed faculty members are deeply engaged in both teaching and research, ensuring a vibrant academic atmosphere for students.
In 2019-20, the department proudly introduced the Master of Science (M.Sc.) program in Mathematics and Computing, complemented by Ph.D. programs spanning various research areas of Mathematics. Efforts are underway to develop robust infrastructure to foster innovative research opportunities and integrate modern teaching methodologies at the new campus.
The department is dedicated to instilling a spirit of curiosity, critical thinking, and a deep appreciation for mathematics among students. Prospective participants are welcomed to join the department, embarking on a journey of intellectual exploration and discovery at IIT Bhilai.
