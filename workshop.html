About the Workshop

The main purpose of the workshop is to foster mathematical cooperation in the above areas to further ther interaction among the researchers in India, in particular in and around Bhilai, Durg. The primary emphasis of the workshop will be on training Ph.D. students, postdoctoral fellows, and other young researchers, and promoting collaborative research involving all the potential participants. The relevant themes from harmonic mappings & partial differential equations will be introduced to the participants through series of lectures on these topics by renowned experts.
