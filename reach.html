How to reach
IIT Bhilai

IIT Bhilai’s campus is situated in Kutelabhata village of Durg district

The address is as follows: IIT Bhilai, Kutelabhata Village, Durg District, Bhilai-49001, Chhattisgarh. One can reach Bhilai via air, train, or road.
Transportation

Swami Vivekanand Airport, Raipur, is the nearest airport to the city of Bhilai, roughly 48 km away, and it takes nearly 1.5 hours to reach. The airport is 53 km away from IIT Bhilai’s campus.

After arriving at the airport, one can use other modes of transportation to reach the campus, such as: Intercity buses from Raipur to Bhilai are frequent, so one can take a bus from Raipur to Bhilai. One can also take an authorized or prepaid taxi from the airport directly to the campus.

Durg Railway Junction is 9 km from the campus and takes 15-20 minutes to reach. After arriving at the railway station, various transport facilities like autorickshaws can be availed to reach the campus.

The campus is 5 km away from the Great Eastern Highway. The campus is wellconnected by roadways. Durg Bus Stand is 8.2 km away from the campus.
